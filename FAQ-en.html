General
Where can I find current offers and how can I order them?

You can find the current offers and promotions via the “Promotions” tile on the homepage, via the corresponding link in the header, or when searching for items through the respective feature on the product.

How can I print labels?

To print a list of labels for your warehouse, there are two options:

1. Select the list “Label List” via “My Lists” in the header and click on the printer icon

2. Select any list and download a PDF of the associated labels via the download icon.

Where can I find my order history?

You can find an overview of your order history both on the homepage and under “My Account” in the black area of your dashboard.

User Management
I would like to have an overview of the orders from my different businesses or customer numbers – is that possible?

For this, you need a user with a central purchasing function. Please contact your consultant in the internal or external sales team.

How can I create a webshop account for additional users and how do their permissions differ?

As a customer administrator, you can easily create additional users via the “My Account” section – “User Management”. You can define whether your employees may only order from specific lists (Purchaser) or from the entire assortment (Full Access), whether they can see prices or statistics, and whether they can edit recipes. Use the shop’s features to give your team the opportunity to place orders independently.

How can I manage user data?

You can manage your user data under “My Account” in the “Personal Information” section. Only payment information and logistical information (e.g. changing the delivery address) must be communicated to us by phone or during a personal meeting with your consultant.

How can I add another email address for the order confirmation?

Under “My Account” – “Personal Information” you can add additional email addresses for order confirmations. Click on “Add Recipient”, enter the corresponding address, and then save your changes.

Order
How can I quickly place my order?

If you want to order quickly using item numbers, you can enter them in the search bar (please note that you do not confirm the item number with Enter) and the expanded search field will automatically open. There you can add the item directly to your cart using the green shopping cart icon.

Where can I add an order or item note?

You can add an order note in the shopping cart by clicking on the pencil icon .

Will items remain in the shopping cart if I don’t submit the order?

The items remain in the cart. An exception is data maintenance on our side, if an item is no longer available or inactive. In this case, the item will remain in the cart but only as an empty line. You must manually delete this line to be able to submit the cart. This is about transparency: we want you to notice if items you had previously added to your cart are no longer available.

What is a direct shipment item, stock item, and order item?

Stock items are items we keep in stock and can deliver to you at any time. Order items are items that complement our assortment and that we are happy to procure for you, but which we do not always keep in stock. Direct shipment items are items that are shipped directly to you via parcel service (please note that delivery dates may differ).

Why is my shopping cart divided?

If you want to purchase an item that is only available after your requested delivery date, it will be placed in a new partial cart with its own expected delivery date.
Please note: Delivery will only take place once the delivery conditions are met.

Lists
What are the order set and "My Lists"? How do I work with them?
  • The order set contains your frequently ordered items, which we compile for you from all sales channels. In the list management section, you can add or remove items from the order set.
  • “My Lists” are lists you create yourself in list management. Map your warehouse down to the exact storage location, plan an event in advance, or create recurring order lists.
How can I create my order set as a file?

You can download your order set in the list management as a .csv file via the download icon .

How do I order using labels?

In list management, you have the option to print labels (barcodes) for your created lists. With our scanning function in the Service-Bund Webshop App (available in the Google Play Store and Apple Store), you can scan these labels and thus speed up your ordering process.

What does the "Standard" field mean and how do I work with it?

In the Standard field in the lists, you define the quantity per item that you regularly order. Using the green “add to cart” button, you can add all stored standard quantities in the list to the shopping cart with a single click.

How can I sort my list?
  • All Lists

    Your lists are always displayed to you in the header, in the list management, and on your dashboard in the following order:

    1. Order Set
    2. Contracts (if available)
    3. Label List (if available)
    4. Created lists in alphanumeric order

    A change to this order is not possible.

  • Individual Lists
    • Newly created list:
      When creating a list, the added items are initially sorted chronologically: the first added item appears at the top, and the last added item appears at the bottom.
    • Sort list:
      You can sort your list differently using the gray input field to the left of the star button. Enter numbers in the input fields and then click the sort button located above your list on the left. In the expanded drop-down menu, you can choose either “Ascending” or “Descending”. We recommend numbers in the format 0010.
    • Reorganize list:
      If you want to insert items into an already sorted list, you can select the last option in the drop-down menu “Reorganize”. With this function, all numbering will be replaced by consecutive numbers in the 0010 format. Entries outside this format (e.g. “1, 2, 3” or “K01, K02, K03”) will be overwritten, and all items will be sorted ascending according to the updated numbering.

      For example, if you want to insert an item between numbers 0040 and 0050, you should enter a number between 0040 and 0050 (e.g. 0045) into the input field. After “Reorganize”, the entries will be consecutively sorted in ascending order in the 0010 format.

I am in the standard search but only see my order set items. How do I switch to “Catalog”/full assortment?

Above the item list, there is a dropdown menu where you can filter by lists or the full catalog. If you have selected the order set or a list, this filter will remain for the next search queries. Only when you switch back to “Full Catalog” will the next search take place in the entire catalog again.

How is the Shop App installed?

Android devices

On Android devices, the installation link automatically appears after a few seconds once the webshop has been opened there.

Apple devices

On Apple devices, use the "Add to Home Screen" option via the share button at the right edge of the URL bar.

How does offline mode work?
  • When logging into the app for the first time, a synchronization takes place that stores important information for offline use.
    This may take a few minutes. Please do not close the app during this time and make sure the screen remains active.
  • Not all functions are available in offline mode. To submit your order, you must have an active internet connection.
